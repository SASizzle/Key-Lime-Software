# Press Release

## KLS Contact Finder Helps Oklahoma Outreach Work Stay Local, Organized, and Actionable

KLS Contact Finder is a local-first outreach contact management system built for Oklahoma high school workflows. It helps a single operator keep a working list of schools, discover public-facing school websites, scrape likely staff contacts, review the best candidates, and track outreach history over time.

Instead of treating scraping as a one-time data grab, KLS Contact Finder supports the full working cycle:

- maintain a school list
- review website quality and scrape readiness
- gather likely contacts
- confirm the best official contact
- log follow-up activity
- export filtered working lists when needed

## Product Purpose

The product exists to reduce the manual overhead of outreach preparation. Many school websites are inconsistent, incomplete, or difficult to navigate. KLS Contact Finder brings those steps into one local workflow so the operator can move from "Which site is correct?" to "Who should I contact next?" with less friction.

## Target User

KLS Contact Finder is designed primarily for:

- a single local outreach operator
- a staff member maintaining a working contact database
- a user who needs reviewable results instead of fully automated outreach decisions

It is especially suited to workflows where accuracy, traceability, and iterative cleanup matter more than large-scale automation.

## Value Proposition

KLS Contact Finder delivers value in five practical ways:

1. It keeps data local by storing records in a local SQLite database instead of requiring a shared cloud service.
2. It combines school management, scraping, review, outreach logging, and export in one tool.
3. It helps recover useful contacts faster through Quick Review and candidate scoring.
4. It preserves human judgment by requiring review of scraped results instead of pretending the scraper is perfect.
5. It supports day-to-day use through packaged Windows demo builds and a browser-based interface.

## Current Release Highlights

- School list dashboard with search and scrape readiness
- Website autodiscovery for missing school sites
- Single-school and batch scraping
- Contact candidate extraction, ranking, and cleanup
- Quick Review and Full Review workflows
- Outreach history and contact status tracking
- CSV import plus CSV/XLSX export
- Local settings and Windows packaging support

## Current Product Boundaries

KLS Contact Finder is not positioned as:

- a multi-user CRM
- a cloud-hosted collaboration platform
- a guaranteed contact verification service
- a fully autonomous outreach bot

It is a local working tool that helps a human operator make faster, better-informed outreach decisions.
